ingredients:
  - 1 small head cauliflower (approximately 4 cups processed)
  - 1 onion, finely diced
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - 1 teaspoon olive oil
  - ¼ teaspoon salt
  - 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  - 1½ tablespoons cumin
  - 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  - 1 tablespoon lime juice
  - ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  - pinch of cayenne pepper

## Method

1. Pulse cauliflower florets in a food processor for 5–6 seconds until broken down to rice-sized pieces.

2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté diced onion and garlic for 3 minutes until softened.

3. Add cauliflower rice, salt, and chopped tomatoes. Cover and cook for 5–7 minutes until cauliflower softens.

4. Stir in cumin, smoked paprika, lime juice, and cayenne. Cook until tender but not mushy.

5. Fold in fresh cilantro just before serving.
