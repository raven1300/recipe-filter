## Method

1. Preheat oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.

2. Heat a frying pan over medium heat with some olive oil and cook the beef mince until browned.

3. Add the Mingle Taco Seasoning and tomato paste, cook for a few minutes until fragrant and the tomato paste deepens.

4. Add a splash of water. Stir well and simmer for 2–3 minutes until beef is coated.

5. Spoon the beef mixture onto half of each tortilla and sprinkle with grated cheese. Fold the tortillas over to create tacos.

6. Place onto the tray and bake for 10–12 minutes until the tortillas are golden and crispy and the cheese has melted.

7. Top with diced tomato, red onion, coriander, and any optional toppings before serving.
