## Method

1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook sausages until golden, then remove.

2. Add remaining oil, garlic, and onion; cook 2 minutes. Add capsicum and cook 2 more minutes until onion is translucent.

3. Add rice, chicken stock, paprika, salt, pepper, and sausage; stir to combine. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a gentle simmer.

4. Cover and cook 20 minutes.

5. Remove from heat, quickly add peas, cover again, and rest 10 minutes.

6. Add parsley and fluff rice with a fork before serving.
