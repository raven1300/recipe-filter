  - 1 cup quinoa
  - 480ml water
  - 1 medium red onion, chopped
  - 3 cloves garlic (1½ teaspoons minced)
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - 1 can no-salt-added diced tomatoes (405g)
  - 1 can tomato sauce (425g)
  - ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  - ¼ teaspoon lemon pepper
  - ½ teaspoon oregano
  - ½ teaspoon basil
  - ½ teaspoon parsley
  - 1 cup fat-free or reduced-fat mozzarella cheese
  - ⅓ cup whole wheat bread crumbs
  - ½ cup grated parmesan and romano cheese

## Method

1. Add quinoa and water to a stovetop pan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer 15–20 minutes until water is absorbed. Remove from heat.

2. Chop the red onion and garlic. Heat olive oil in another pan over medium heat and cook onion and garlic until browned.

3. Reduce heat to low-medium. Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, spices, cooked quinoa, and diced raw chicken. Simmer for 5 minutes.

4. Coat a baking dish with non-stick spray. Add half the mixture, then top with half the bread crumbs, mozzarella, and parmesan.

5. Add the remaining mixture and top with the remaining bread crumbs and cheeses.

6. Cover with foil and bake at 190°C for 15 minutes.

7. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
