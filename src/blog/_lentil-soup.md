## Method

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add garlic and onion; cook for 2 minutes.

2. Add celery and carrot; cook for 7–10 minutes until softened.

3. Add lentils, crushed tomato, stock, cumin, coriander, paprika, and bay leaves. Stir to combine.

4. Bring to a simmer, skimming off any surface scum. Cover and simmer for 35–40 minutes until lentils are soft.

5. Remove bay leaves.

6. Use a stick blender to partially blend the soup to your preferred consistency, or transfer a portion to a blender.

7. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper, then stir in lemon zest and juice. Garnish with parsley and serve with warm bread.
