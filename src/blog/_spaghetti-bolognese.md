  - 2 tbsp tomato paste
  - 2 tsp white sugar

## Method

1. Heat oil in large pot over medium-high heat; add onion and garlic, cook 3 minutes until softened.

2. Increase heat to high, add beef, breaking apart while cooking until browned.

3. Add red wine, simmer 1 minute while scraping pot bottom until alcohol smell dissipates.

4. Add remaining sauce ingredients, bring to simmer, reduce to medium heat, cook 20-30 minutes uncovered, stirring occasionally.

5. Taste and adjust salt.

6. Boil salted water, cook pasta one minute under package directions.

7. Reserve 1 mug pasta water, then drain pasta.

8. Add pasta to sauce with ½ cup reserved water over medium heat; toss 1-2 minutes until coated.

9. Divide into bowls and garnish with parmesan and parsley.
