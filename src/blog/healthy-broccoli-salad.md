  - 5 cups broccoli florets

  - ½ cup red onion, chopped
  - ½ cup raisins
  - ⅓ cup sunflower seeds
  - ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
  - 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  - 3 tablespoons honey (or maple syrup)
  - 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  - 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  - ½ teaspoon salt
  - ¼ teaspoon black pepper

## Method

1. Whisk together Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, honey, lemon juice, salt, and pepper until creamy. Adjust consistency with a little olive oil or water as needed.

2. Combine broccoli, grapes, red onion, raisins, and sunflower seeds in a large bowl.

3. Add the dressing and toss until everything is evenly coated. Season to taste.

4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving (can be prepared up to 24 hours in advance).
