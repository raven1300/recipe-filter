ingredients:
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - ½ onion, diced
  - 250g (8oz) chicken thigh or breast, cut into bite-size pieces
  - 2.5 cups frozen diced vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, peppers)
  - 1.5 cups risoni, uncooked
  - 1.5 tbsp flour
  - 2.25 cups milk
  - 2 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  - 3 cups baby spinach
  - ¾ cup parmesan, finely grated
  - ¼ tsp salt
  - ¼ tsp black pepper
  - Additional parmesan and parsley for garnish

## Method

1. Heat oil in large deep pan over high heat.

2. Add garlic and onion; cook 1 minute. Add chicken; sear 2 minutes.

3. Add frozen vegetables and flour; stir until incorporated.

4. Add risoni, stock, and milk; stir well.

5. Simmer uncovered 7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until risoni is nearly tender.

6. Add parmesan, spinach, salt, and pepper; stir until spinach wilts.

7. Serve immediately garnished with parsley and additional parmesan.
