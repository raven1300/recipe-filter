ingredients:
  - 1½ tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 medium onion, chopped
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 450g lean ground beef
  - 5 cups cauliflower florets
  - ½ cup almond milk
  - ⅓ cup paleo-approved mayonnaise
  - 1½ tablespoons arrowroot flour
  - 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  - 4 bacon slices, cooked and crumbled
  - 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  - salt and black pepper to taste
---

## Method

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.

3. Brown ground beef until no longer pink. Season with salt and pepper.

4. Preheat oven to 175°C.

5. Combine cauliflower florets and beef mixture in a greased baking dish.

6. Whisk together almond milk, mayonnaise, arrowroot flour, and nutritional yeast.

7. Pour sauce over the cauliflower mixture and toss to coat.

8. Bake for 30–40 minutes, stirring 2–3 times, until cauliflower is tender.

9. Top with crumbled bacon and chopped parsley before serving.
