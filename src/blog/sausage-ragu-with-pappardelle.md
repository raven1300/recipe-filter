## Method

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan-forced).

2. Heat oil in a large oven-safe pot over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, carrot, celery, and fennel seeds. Cook for 8 minutes until the carrot softens.

3. Increase heat to high. Add sausage meat, breaking it up as it cooks until browned.

4. Add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute. Pour in wine and simmer for 2 minutes until reduced by half, scraping the bottom of the pot clean.

5. Add crushed tomato, stock, thyme, bay leaves, salt, pepper, and chilli flakes if using. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer. Cover and bake in the oven for 1 hour.

6. Remove from oven and mash the meat finer with a potato masher. Cover and return to the oven for a further 45 minutes.

7. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.

8. Cook pappardelle according to packet directions. Reserve 1½ cups of pasta cooking water before draining.

9. Add drained pasta to the ragu pot with ½ cup of the reserved cooking water. Toss until the sauce coats every strand, adding more cooking water as needed.

10. Plate in warm bowls and garnish with grated parmesan and parsley.
