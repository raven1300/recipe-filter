## Method

1. Preheat oven to 200°C.

2. Cut broccoli into florets and rinse thoroughly, then pat dry.

3. In a large bowl, toss broccoli with minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

4. Spread mixture in a single layer on a rimmed baking sheet.

5. Roast for approximately 15 minutes until edges are crispy and browned, flipping halfway through and adding red pepper flakes at that point if desired.

6. Remove from oven, drizzle with fresh lemon juice, and serve immediately.
