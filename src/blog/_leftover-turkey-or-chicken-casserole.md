  - 9 ounces cooked turkey or chicken
  - 2 cups broccoli (fresh or thawed)
  - ½ can 98% fat-free cream of chicken soup
  - 5.3 ounces plain Greek yogurt
  - 1 cup reduced-fat Swiss cheese, shredded
  - 2½ servings stuffing mix
  - 4 ounces hot water
  - 1-2 tablespoons bacon bits (optional)

## Method

1. Place cooked turkey or chicken at the bottom of a baking dish.

2. Layer broccoli on top of the meat.

3. Mix cream of chicken soup, Greek yogurt, and Swiss cheese together, then spread over the broccoli.

4. Combine stuffing mix with hot water and stir to hydrate.

5. Spread the stuffing mixture over the cheese layer.

6. Top with bacon bits if desired.

7. Bake at 175°C for 30–35 minutes until heated through and the top is golden.
