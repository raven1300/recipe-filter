## Method

1. Preheat oven to 200°C and prep all vegetables.

2. Brown sausage in a large cast-iron skillet over high heat, chopping into 1-inch pieces. Cook 2–3 minutes until golden, then remove and set aside, leaving the grease in the pan.

3. Add onion, bell pepper, and jalapeño to the skillet over medium-high heat. Sauté for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.

4. Add garlic, salt, pepper, paprika, chili powder, oregano, and cumin. Sauté for 3–5 minutes until vegetables begin to brown at the edges.

5. Reduce heat to medium-low. Stir in marinara, diced chiles, and cooked sausage. Bring to a gentle boil.

6. Crack eggs directly onto the surface of the sauce. Transfer the entire skillet to the oven for 10–12 minutes.

7. Remove from oven, drizzle with crema if desired, add optional toppings, and serve family-style.
