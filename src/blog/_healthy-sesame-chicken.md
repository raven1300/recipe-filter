ingredients:
  - 900g chicken thighs (or chicken breast)
  - 2 tablespoons corn starch
  - salt to taste
  - black pepper to taste
  - 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil
  - 5 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
  - 1-2 teaspoons hot sauce (optional)
  - 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  - 2 tablespoons honey
  - 1½ teaspoons ground ginger
  - 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  - 1 tablespoon corn starch (for sauce)
  - 2 tablespoons cold water
---

## Method

1. Mix the corn starch with cold water until smooth, then combine with remaining sauce ingredients (soy sauce, hot sauce, garlic, honey, ginger, sesame seeds) in a bowl and mix thoroughly.

2. Trim fat from the chicken and cut into bite-sized pieces.

3. Toss chicken pieces with corn starch, salt, and black pepper until evenly coated.

4. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat.

5. Add the coated chicken and cook for a few minutes until browned on one side.

6. Flip the chicken and cook until browned on the other side.

7. Pour the sauce over the chicken and simmer on low heat until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken.
