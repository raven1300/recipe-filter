## Method

1. Mix sauce ingredients (soy sauces, oyster sauce, Chinese cooking wine, sugar, white pepper) in a small bowl and set aside.

2. Scramble eggs using sesame oil in a wok or non-stick pan over medium-high heat, stirring gently until softly cooked. Remove to a plate.

3. Heat vegetable oil in the same pan over high heat. Add onion and garlic for 30 seconds. Add beef, breaking it up, for 2 minutes until no raw beef remains. Add 2 tablespoons of sauce and cook 1 minute.

4. Add frozen vegetables and cook 2 minutes until beef is nicely caramelised.

5. Add rice and remaining sauce. Toss for 2 minutes until sauce disperses and rice begins to caramelise.

6. Add scrambled eggs and green onion, toss briefly, then serve.
