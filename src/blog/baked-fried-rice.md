## Method

1. Preheat oven to 200°C/390°F (180°C fan).

2. Mix rice, stock, soy sauce, cooking wine, garlic, frozen vegetables, and white pepper together in a baking dish (about 22×32cm); spread rice evenly.

3. Scatter bacon or ham across the surface.

4. Cover tightly with foil and bake for 40 minutes.

5. Remove foil and bake uncovered for 15 minutes until bacon browns.

6. Cover loosely with foil and let rest 10 minutes.

7. Add green onion and sesame oil, fluff with a fork, and serve.
