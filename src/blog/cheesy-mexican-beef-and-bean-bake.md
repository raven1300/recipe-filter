## Method

1. Preheat oven to 200°C/400°F (180°C fan).

2. Sauté onion, garlic, and capsicum in olive oil over medium-high heat for 3 minutes.

3. Add beef and cook until no raw meat remains; add all spices and cook 1 minute more.

4. Add kidney beans and passata; stir and bring to a simmer.

5. Transfer to a baking dish. Scatter jalapeño over the surface and top evenly with shredded cheese.

6. Bake 15 minutes uncovered until cheese is golden and bubbling.

7. Serve with corn chips or tortillas and toppings such as guacamole, sour cream, and salsa.
