## Method

1. Process chillies, onion, garlic, lemongrass, galangal, and ginger in a food processor until a fine paste forms.

2. Heat oil in a large pot; brown beef in batches, then remove.

3. Lower heat to medium-low; cook spice paste 2-3 minutes until darkened.

4. Add remaining curry ingredients and beef; stir to combine.

5. Bring to simmer, then reduce heat to low/medium-low for a gentle bubble.

6. Cover and simmer for 1 hour 15 minutes.

7. Check beef tenderness; continue cooking if needed.

8. Turn heat to medium and reduce sauce 30-40 minutes, stirring frequently, until it coats the beef.

9. Serve with coconut rice when beef is fall-apart tender.
