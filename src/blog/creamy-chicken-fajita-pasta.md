  - 500g chicken breast, cubed
  - 1 tsp olive oil
  - 1½ tbsp Mingle Fajita Seasoning, for chicken
  - 1 red capsicum, sliced
  - 1 green capsicum, sliced
  - 1 red onion, diced
  - 300g pasta
  - ½ cup cream
  - 1 cup chicken stock
  - 1 heaped tbsp Mingle Fajita Seasoning, for sauce
  - 30g parmesan, grated
  - extra parmesan and parsley to serve (optional)

## Method

1. Cook pasta according to packet instructions in salted boiling water. Drain and set aside, reserving a splash of pasta water.

2. Sauté onion and capsicum for 3–4 minutes until slightly softened.

3. Add chicken and Mingle Fajita Seasoning. Cook until chicken is cooked through.

4. Add chicken stock, cream, Mingle Fajita Seasoning, and parmesan to the pan. Stir and simmer for 2–3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

5. Add the cooked pasta, tossing everything together. Add a splash of reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce and make it glossy.

6. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley and extra parmesan.
