## Method

1. In a small pot over low heat, whisk together butter, buffalo wing sauce, and Mingle Chipotle Seasoning. Stir until silky and well combined.

2. Place the shredded chicken in a bowl and pour the chipotle mixture over the top. Toss well until every piece of chicken is fully coated.

3. Use a bread knife to slice all the rolls at once through the middle, keeping the top and bottom halves connected.

4. Evenly spread the chipotle chicken over the bottom half and top with a generous layer of cheese. Pop the lids back on and brush the tops with garlic herb butter.

5. Bake at 180°C for 10–15 minutes until the tops are golden and crispy and the cheese has melted.
