  - 2½ tbsp soy sauce
  - 3 tbsp honey
  - 1½ tbsp oyster sauce (or hoisin)
  - 1½ tbsp Chinese cooking wine, mirin, or dry sherry
  - 2 tbsp water
  - 1 tsp black pepper
  - 2 tbsp peanut, vegetable, or canola oil
  - 1 garlic clove, minced
  - ½ onion, sliced
  - 500g thinly sliced beef (tenderloin, flank, sirloin, or similar)

## Method

1. Mix all sauce ingredients in a bowl.

2. Heat oil in wok or large skillet over high heat until smoking.

3. Add onion and garlic; cook 1 minute until translucent, stirring constantly.

4. Add beef and stir fry 1 minute until cooked to preference; remove to bowl.

5. Reduce heat to medium-high, pour in sauce; simmer 1 minute until syrupy.

6. Return beef and onion to wok with any pooled juices; toss 1 minute until warm.

7. Serve immediately with rice.
