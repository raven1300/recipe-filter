## Method

1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add ground meat and break it up.

2. Add the peppers, onion, and garlic and stir to combine.

3. Brown the meat for 5–8 minutes, stirring constantly.

4. Reduce heat to low. Mix in chili powder, cumin, diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, and half the enchilada sauce.

5. Pour the remaining enchilada sauce into a 9×13 baking dish.

6. Spread refried beans over the mini tortillas, then place them in the dish over the sauce, overlapping as needed.

7. Pour the meat mixture over the tortillas.

8. Top with Mexican cheese blend.

9. Bake at 175°C for 30–35 minutes until cheese is melted and edges are bubbling. Cover with foil partway through if needed to prevent burning.
