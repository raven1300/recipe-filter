  - ¼ cup onion, finely diced
  - 4 garlic cloves, minced

  - ¾ cup roasted unsalted peanuts, chopped
  - 2 tsp kecap manis

  - 2 tbsp peanut butter
  - 2 tbsp lime juice
  - 3 makrut lime leaves
  - 1 lemongrass stalk

## Method

1. Combine satay seasoning ingredients in a small bowl.

2. Marinate chicken with 3½ tablespoons satay seasoning and grated onion for at least 3 hours, preferably overnight.

3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet over high heat; cook chicken in two batches until browned, transfer to bowl.

4. Turn heat to medium, add 1 tbsp oil; sauté chilli, diced onion, and garlic until onion is translucent (about 2 minutes).

5. Add remaining satay seasoning and cook 1 minute.

6. Transfer to blender with chicken stock and ½ cup peanuts; puree until smooth.

7. Return mixture to skillet; add remaining ¼ cup peanuts, kecap manis, soy sauce, coconut milk, and peanut butter.

8. Crush and add makrut lime leaves and lemongrass; add cooked chicken.

9. Bring to simmer over medium-low heat for 15 minutes until thickened.

10. Add lime juice to taste; serve with rice, garnished with peanuts, cilantro, and chilli.
