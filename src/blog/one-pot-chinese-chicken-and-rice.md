## Method

1. Combine garlic, Chinese cooking wine, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, and white pepper in a large bowl. Add chicken and marinate for at least 10 minutes (or overnight).

2. Cut Chinese broccoli stems into 2.5cm pieces; cut leafy parts into 5cm pieces. Keep separate.

3. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook chicken until golden, about 90 seconds per side. Remove and set aside, reserving the marinade.

4. In the same pot, add onion, garlic, and ginger; cook 2 minutes until lightly golden.

5. Stir in rice to coat with the oil. Add broth, reserved marinade, and broccoli stems.

6. Place chicken on top with any resting juices. Cover and simmer on medium heat for 12–15 minutes until liquid is absorbed.

7. Remove from heat. Scatter broccoli leaves across the surface and cover for 10 minutes to steam.

8. Toss rice to combine and serve garnished with sesame seeds and sliced green onions.
