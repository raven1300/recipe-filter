  - 1½ lb chicken breast (700g)
  - 1½ lb broccoli (700g)
  - ½ stick butter (50g)
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - ¾ cup Parmesan, grated (75g)
  - 1 cup heavy cream (250ml)
  - salt, pepper, and nutmeg
  - 1 cup mozzarella, grated (100g)

## Method

1. Cook chicken breast in a pan over medium-high heat until cooked through. Slice or shred and set aside.

2. Place broccoli florets in a baking dish and top with the cooked chicken.

3. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt butter and sauté garlic for 1 minute. Add cream and Parmesan and stir until slightly thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg.

4. Pour the Alfredo sauce over the chicken and broccoli. Top with grated mozzarella.

5. Bake at 190°C (374°F) for 40 minutes until bubbling and golden on top.
