  - 500g/1 lb chicken thighs, sliced
  - 2.5 tbsp curry powder
  - 270 ml/1.25 cups full-fat coconut milk
  - 1.5 cups (375 ml) low-sodium chicken stock
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ¾ cup frozen peas
  - ¼ cup coriander/cilantro leaves, chopped

## Method

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook garlic, ginger, and onion for 3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent.

2. Add chicken and cook until it changes from pink to white.

3. Add curry powder and cook for 2 minutes, stirring to coat the chicken.

4. Add coconut milk and chicken stock. Stir to combine, then lower heat to medium and simmer rapidly for 10 minutes until the sauce reduces and thickens.

5. Add peas and salt. Cook for a further 2 minutes. Taste and adjust salt as needed.

6. Garnish with coriander and serve over rice, noodles, or mashed potato.
