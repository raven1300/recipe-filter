## Method

1. Make the whipped ricotta: whisk ricotta, milk, and parmesan together for 10–15 seconds until it reaches a softly whipped cream consistency. Set aside.

2. Heat reserved sun-dried tomato oil in a large pot over high heat. Cook diced chicken for 3 minutes until sealed. Remove and set aside.

3. In the same pot, cook onion and garlic for 3 minutes until translucent. Add tomato paste and sun-dried tomatoes; cook 1 minute.

4. Add white wine and simmer for 1–2 minutes until mostly evaporated.

5. Add pasta, returned chicken, stock, canned tomato, salt, and pepper. Simmer uncovered for 15 minutes, stirring frequently, until liquid is absorbed and pasta is al dente.

6. Stir in fresh basil and transfer to a serving bowl.

7. Top with dollops of whipped ricotta, smearing them through the pasta before eating.
