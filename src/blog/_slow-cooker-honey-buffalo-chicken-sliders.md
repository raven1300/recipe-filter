  - 2 lb / 1 kg chicken breast (boneless skinless)
  - 4 tbsp butter
  - ¾ cup hot sauce
  - ¼ cup + 2 tbsp honey
  - 2 tbsp brown sugar
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
  - ½ tsp garlic powder
  - ½ tsp paprika
  - ½ tsp dried thyme
  - 1 tsp salt
  - Black pepper to taste
  - 1½ tbsp cornstarch
  - 7 slider rolls

## Method

1. Place chicken in a single layer in the slow cooker; sprinkle with garlic powder, paprika, thyme, salt, and pepper. Add remaining sauce ingredients around the chicken (not on top). Cook on LOW for 4 hours.

2. Remove chicken from slow cooker and shred roughly with two forks.

3. Mix cornstarch with 2 tbsp water and stir into the sauce in the slow cooker. Simmer on sauté/high setting for 5 minutes until thickened to a syrup consistency.

4. Return shredded chicken to the sauce and toss to coat; keep warm.

5. Split rolls in half and toast if desired.

6. Layer lettuce on the bottom half of each roll and top generously with chicken. Serve warm.
