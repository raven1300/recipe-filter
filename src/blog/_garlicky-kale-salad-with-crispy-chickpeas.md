## Method

1. Preheat oven to 190°C. Separate garlic cloves, keeping skins on.

2. Toss dried chickpeas with oil, a pinch of salt, and tandoori masala spice blend until coated.

3. Roast garlic and seasoned chickpeas together for 15–20 minutes until garlic is browned. Remove garlic and continue roasting chickpeas for another 10–15 minutes until crispy.

4. Squeeze roasted garlic cloves out of their skins into a bowl. Whisk with tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, maple syrup, salt, and pepper. Thin with hot water to a pourable consistency.

5. Massage kale with a little lemon juice and olive oil until slightly softened.

6. Toss kale with desired amount of dressing, top with crispy chickpeas, and serve fresh.
