  - 1-2 servings pepperoni slices (optional)
  - 6 large egg whites

  - 2 cans pizza sauce (425g each)
  - 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  - 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  - 225g reduced-fat or fat-free pizza cheese

## Method

1. Cook pasta for approximately 5 minutes until just before fully done (al dente).

2. Brown the ground meat in a skillet and drain excess fat if needed.

3. Combine all ingredients except the cheese in a large bowl and mix well.

4. Coat the slow cooker with non-stick spray or use a liner.

5. Add a layer of the mixture to the slow cooker, then top with a layer of cheese. Repeat for a total of 3 layers, finishing with cheese on top.

6. Cover and cook on low heat for 3.5–4 hours.
