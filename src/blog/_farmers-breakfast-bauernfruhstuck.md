  - salt and pepper to taste
  - 1 tablespoon finely sliced chives

## Method

1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add sliced potatoes, lower heat to a simmer, and cook for 13–15 minutes until tender. Drain.

2. In a large frying pan, sauté bacon over medium-high heat for 2 minutes. Add olive oil and the cooked potatoes, then fry, stirring regularly, until the potatoes are golden brown, about 10 minutes.

3. Whisk eggs with milk, oregano, salt, and pepper until combined.

4. Reduce heat to medium-low and pour the egg mixture over the golden potatoes. Gently stir with a wooden spoon until the eggs are just cooked, about 2–3 minutes.

5. Sprinkle with chives and serve immediately.
