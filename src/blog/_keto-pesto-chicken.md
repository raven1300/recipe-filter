ingredients:
  - 900g chicken breast tenders
  - 1 teaspoon sea salt
  - ½ teaspoon cracked black pepper
  - 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  - 2 tablespoons avocado oil
  - 1 head broccoli (about 450g), cut into florets
  - 280g grape tomatoes
  - ½ cup chicken broth
  - 2 jars basil pesto (185g each)
  - ¼ cup shredded parmesan
---

## Method

1. Slice chicken tenders into 1-inch pieces and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

2. Heat a large skillet with avocado oil. Sear the chicken in a single layer for 3 minutes per side until golden-brown. Remove and set aside.

3. Add chicken broth to the hot pan and scrape up any browned bits. Add broccoli and grape tomatoes, cover, and steam for 2 minutes.

4. Turn off the heat. Return chicken to the pan, stir in pesto until everything is coated, top with parmesan, and serve.
