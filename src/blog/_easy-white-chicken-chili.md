ingredients:
  - 1 yellow onion
  - 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - 1.35kg chicken breast
  - 950ml bone broth
  - 110g green chiles, diced
  - 110g jalapeños, diced
  - 2 cups corn (canned or frozen)
  - 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  - 1 teaspoon oregano
  - ½ teaspoon chili powder
  - ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  - ½ teaspoon salt
  - black pepper to taste
  - 2 cans white beans (Great Northern, 435g each, drained and rinsed)
  - 1 container Greek yogurt (150g / 5.3 oz)
  - 110g cream cheese
---

## Method

1. Optionally line the slow cooker for easy cleanup.

2. Sauté the onion and garlic with olive oil until softened.

3. Trim fat from the chicken breast.

4. Add all ingredients except Greek yogurt and cream cheese to the slow cooker and mix well.

5. Cover and cook on low for 5–6 hours or on high for 3–4 hours.

6. Remove chicken and shred it, then return to the pot.

7. Stir in Greek yogurt and cream cheese and continue cooking until the chili is creamy and well combined.
