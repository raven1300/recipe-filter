ingredients:
  - 900g chicken breast, chopped
  - 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed (435g)
  - 680g sweet potatoes, chopped
  - 225g baby or chopped carrots
  - 1 cup frozen corn
  - 1 can fire-roasted diced tomatoes (405g)
  - 2 tablespoons coconut flour
  - 1½ teaspoons minced garlic
  - 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  - ¼ teaspoon salt
  - ½ teaspoon black pepper
  - 2 tablespoons BBQ sauce
  - ½ white onion, chopped
  - 950ml chicken bone broth
---

## Method

1. Add all ingredients to the slow cooker and mix everything together.

2. Place the lid on and cook on low for 7–8 hours or on high for 3–4 hours, stirring occasionally if you are around.
