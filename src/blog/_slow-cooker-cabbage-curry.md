ingredients:
  - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 2 teaspoons grated ginger
  - 1 onion, thinly sliced
  - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  - 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  - 1 teaspoon chili powder
  - ½ teaspoon turmeric
  - 1½ teaspoons garam masala
  - 1 head cabbage, cored and chopped
  - 2 russet potatoes, peeled and chopped
  - 2 plum tomatoes, chopped
  - 1 cup tomato sauce
  - 2 teaspoons salt
  - 1 cup frozen peas

## Method

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook garlic for about 1 minute.

2. Add onion and ginger and cook, stirring, until softened, about 5 minutes.

3. Add cumin, mustard seeds, chili powder, turmeric, and garam masala. Cook, stirring, for 30 seconds.

4. Transfer the mixture to the slow cooker.

5. Add cabbage, potatoes, tomato sauce, and salt and stir well to combine.

6. Add chopped tomatoes and stir again.

7. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours.

8. Stir in frozen peas, cover, and cook for an additional 30 minutes.

9. Serve hot over steamed basmati rice or with warm naan.
