## Method

1. Pulse cauliflower florets in a food processor until they resemble rice. Transfer to a cheesecloth or paper towels and squeeze out as much moisture as possible. Set aside.

2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add garlic and red onion and sauté for 2–3 minutes until softened.

3. Add the chicken and turkey sausage and cook until the chicken is browned and the sausage is lightly caramelized.

4. Stir in the bell pepper, celery, and cherry tomatoes. Season with Cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper. Cook for 3–4 minutes.

5. Add the tomato sauce, tomato paste, and chicken broth. Stir to combine, then add the bay leaves.

6. Fold in the cauliflower rice and shrimp. Cover and simmer over medium-low heat for 15–20 minutes until the shrimp are cooked through and the cauliflower rice has absorbed the sauce.

7. Remove the lid and cook uncovered for a few minutes to allow excess liquid to thicken.

8. Remove bay leaves, adjust seasoning as needed, and garnish with sliced green onions before serving.
