## Method

1. Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F.

2. Heat oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.

3. Cook bacon briefly, then add sausages until golden (3 minutes); remove to bowl.

4. Add chicken; cook until golden; transfer to bowl.

5. Sear prawns 1.5 minutes per side; reserve separately.

6. Add butter, garlic, onion, celery, and capsicum; cook 3-4 minutes until slightly softened.

7. Add rice and stir to coat with oil.

8. Add chicken broth, tomato paste, canned tomato, thyme, and all seasonings; stir well.

9. Add chicken, sausages, and bacon with all liquid; stir to combine.

10. Once bubbles appear across the surface, stir once more, ensure rice is submerged, cover, and transfer to oven.

11. Bake 20 minutes; check rice doneness; continue baking if needed (typically ~30 minutes total).

12. Add prawns and green onions; stir quickly and gently; cover and return to oven for 3 minutes.

13. Remove from oven, gently fluff, and serve with additional green onions if desired.
