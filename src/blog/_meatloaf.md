## Method

1. Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F. Oil or spray a loaf tin.

2. Mix glaze ingredients (ketchup, cider vinegar, brown sugar) in a small bowl and set aside.

3. Place breadcrumbs in a large bowl, grate onion over them, and mix until the breadcrumbs are wet.

4. Add remaining meatloaf ingredients and mix well with hands; form into a loaf shape.

5. Transfer to loaf tin and brush generously with about half the glaze.

6. Bake 45 minutes, then brush with remaining glaze and drain excess fat.

7. Bake 30 minutes more.

8. Stand 10 minutes before turning out and slicing.

9. Serve with ketchup, mashed potatoes, and steamed vegetables.
