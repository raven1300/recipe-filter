  - 20 ounces fajita vegetables (frozen or fresh bell peppers and onion)
  - 1.35kg chicken breast
  - ½ packet taco or fajita seasoning mix
  - 1 ounce lime juice
  - 1 tablespoon honey
  - 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  - 14.5 ounces diced tomatoes
---

## Method

1. Trim fat from the chicken breast.

2. Layer half the fajita vegetables in the slow cooker, then add the chicken.

3. Add seasonings, lime juice, honey, garlic, and diced tomatoes, then top with the remaining vegetables.

4. Mix the ingredients lightly.

5. Cook on high for 3 hours.

6. Remove the chicken and shred it, then return it to the slow cooker.

7. Cook on low for an additional 1 hour before serving.
