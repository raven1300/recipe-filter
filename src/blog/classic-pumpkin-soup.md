## Method

1. Cut pumpkin into 3cm slices, remove skin and seeds, then cut into 4cm chunks.

2. Place pumpkin, onion, garlic, broth, and water in a large pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until pumpkin is tender, about 10 minutes.

3. Remove from heat and blend with a stick blender until completely smooth.

4. Season with salt and pepper, then stir in cream.

5. Ladle into bowls, drizzle with a little extra cream, add a crack of pepper and parsley if desired. Serve with crusty bread.
