## Raven's note

The recipe calls for monkfish, and I substituted in chicken for it.  Feel free to change it back, or not include it at all and keep it vegetarian.

## Method

1. Soak the chilli flakes and desiccated coconut in the boiling water for 10 minutes. Blend with the red onion, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper until smooth.

2. Heat coconut oil in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Fry the cumin seeds, mustard seeds, turmeric, and curry leaves for 1–2 minutes. Add the curry paste and fry for 5 minutes, stirring continuously.

3. Add the lentils, coconut milk, and vegetable stock. Simmer gently for 20 minutes until the lentils soften.

4. Add the chicken, green beans, and spinach. Cook for 8 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.

5. Serve in bowls topped with coriander and lime wedges.
